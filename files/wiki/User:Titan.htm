[edit] My Configuration Files

[edit] xorg.conf

I discovered pretty quickly that very little is needed to get X.Org up and running. As you'll see in the following example, I have a ton of fonts installed -- I just like having choices and having support for a wide variety of fonts -- so I have them listed so X.Org can find them in case Xft isn't used by a program. Now the interesting bit, I have two sections that are all I required to have X.Org start and display with a proper aspect ratio: Device and Screen. What you see in those sections are the absolute minimum needed to have X.Org use the official Nvidia driver without showing a logo and to set my monitor resolution at 1280x960 instead of 1280x1024, as X.Org thought my resolution should be. If the resolution had been proper in the first place, I wouldn't even had to have a Screen section. (I prefer a proper 4:3 over the odd 5:4.)
